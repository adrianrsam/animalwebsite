Lion

The lion is a large feline known for its strength and majestic mane. It is native to Africa and a symbol of courage and power.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest species of the cat family. It is known for its distinctive orange coat with black stripes.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are highly intelligent and known for their strong social bonds.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth, with long necks that allow them to reach high tree leaves.

Zebra

Zebras are known for their unique black-and-white striped coats, which help camouflage them in the wild.
